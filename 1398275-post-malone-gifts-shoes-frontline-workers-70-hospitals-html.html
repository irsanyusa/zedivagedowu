Post Malone gifts shoes to frontline workers in over 70 hospitals

- Post Malone has gifted frontline medical workers 10 000 pairs of shoes during the pandemic

- The rapper gave away the shoes to around 70 different hospitals in the United States

- Social media users all over the world were moved by the kind gesture from the Grammy-nominated artist

The pandemic has been hard for a lot of people so some celebrities have decided to give back to the community. Rapper Post Malone is one of the latest celebrities to do a good deed for the greater good of the public.

The Rockstar hitmaker decided to gift 10 000 pairs of his crocs to frontline workers who are trying to fight the Covid-19 pandemic in hospitals.

The Grammy-nominated artist gifted his shoes to around 70 hospitals across the United States.

Musicians On Call is the organisation through which Post Malone is going to be donated the shoes. The non-profit organisation took to social media to share the news.
