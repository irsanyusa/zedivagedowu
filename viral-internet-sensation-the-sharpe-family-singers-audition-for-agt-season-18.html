The Sharpe Family Singers, an American family music group, have become one of the most popular and influential singing groups in American music history. With their upcoming participation in season 18 of America’s Got Talent, they are poised to captivate a larger audience.

Who Are The Sharpe Family Singers?

At the core of the Sharpe Family Singers are the talented siblings who have honed their skills through years of dedication and hard work. Led by their parents, Ron and Barbara Sharpe, the family has nurtured a deep love for music and Broadway.

Ron and Barbara, Broadway performers and producers themselves, first met onstage during the original Broadway production of Les Misérables, where they played the romantic lead roles of Marius and Cosette. After tying the knot in real life, they decided to share their passion for music and performing with their children.

The Sharpe Family Singers consist of four siblings: Logan, Samantha, Aidan, and Connor. Each member brings a unique voice and personality to the group, creating a harmonious blend of talent that leaves audiences in awe.

In February 2020, during the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, the Sharpe Family Singers turned to TikTok as a means of family bonding. Little did they know that their musical content would quickly gain traction and then lead to their rise to internet fame. Their performances are not only filled with incredible vocal prowess but also infused with a genuine sense of joy and togetherness, showcasing the tight bond they share as a family.

They Are Internet Famous With Viral Videos

Beyond their captivating live performances, the Sharpe Family Singers have gained widespread recognition and adoration through their viral videos. Their renditions of popular Broadway hits, contemporary songs, and also holiday classics have garnered millions of views, cementing their status as internet sensations.

In one of their viral videos, the Sharpe Family Singers captivated audiences with their incredible cover of “We Don’t Talk About Bruno” from the hit Disney movie Encanto. The family’s rendition immediately went viral, garnering over 58 million views to date.

With 1.6 million subscribers on YouTube, 8.5 million followers on TikTok, and 235,000 fans on Facebook, the Sharpe Family Singers have certainly built a substantial online presence. Stay tuned as the Sharpe Family Singers take the stage at AGT and continue to share their musical talents with the world.
