Additional information

Practise

To ensure the quality of achieved knowledge, all our students undergo obligatory 13 weeks internship in the last semester of their degree. They can choose from more than 1500 partners companies of the institute or they can find company of their interest in Czech Republic or abroad

Employment

Within two months of completing their studies, more than 97% of our graduates find employment.

Dormitory

Dorm rooms are available for approximately 150 $ / 116€ a month. Each room is equipped with desks and chairs, wardrobes and beds. A shared kitchen with basic equipment for cooking and heating up or cooling food or drinks: a kitchen unit, cooker, fridge, microwave oven, electric kettle, dishes.

Cafeteria

In our cafeteria, students can choose from a menu on working days (four meals, in summer only three meals). Between 11:00 – 17:00, it is also possible from five quick dishes (four in summer months). In the cafeteria, it is also possible to buy drinks, sweets, fast food meals, snacks, etc.

Laboratories

The Institute of Technology and Business has new laboratories that are used for practical lessons and for reasearch. The focus of the laboratories corresponds to the curriculum engineering, construction, transport and logistics. In laboratories, it is possible to examine the hardness of materials, make various measurements and composition of these materials, or create completely new products using CNC machines. 3D measuring machines and much more.

Erasmus+ programme

Institute of Technology and Business (VSTE) participates in Erasmus+, program enhancing international mobility worldwide, with supporting program of Erasmus Student Network. Each semester, students of VSTE get the opportunity to study or complete internship abroad and gain international experience. Whole program is founded by European Union.

International students

Every year, about 150 students from all around the world travel to VSTE to participate at various international mobility programmes (Erasmus+, Ceepus, bilateral agreements) to improve their language skills and gain study and personal experience. Our active ESN Budweis is an volunteer organisation, which provides trips, sport events, parties and other activities for our international students.
