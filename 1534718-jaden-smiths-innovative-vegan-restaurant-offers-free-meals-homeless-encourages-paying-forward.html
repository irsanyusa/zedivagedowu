  • Social media users have commended Smith's compassion and proactive approach to addressing homelessness through his philanthropic initiatives

Jaden Smith has made a significant impact with his dedication to charity work. In his latest venture, the Again hitmaker has launched a remarkable vegan restaurant to provide free meals for the homeless.

This commendable initiative follows Smith's successful introduction of the I Love You Food Truck, which tirelessly distributed food to those in need in the impoverished area of Skid Row, reports ECR.
