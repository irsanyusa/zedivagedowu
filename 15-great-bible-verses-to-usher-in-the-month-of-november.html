15 great Bible verses to usher in the month of November!

 Posted on May 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  505 words  |  Aldo Pusey

1. Exodus 12:2

"This month shall be the beginning of months for you..."

ADVERTISEMENT

2. Lamentations 3:22–23

"The steadfast love of the Lord never ceases; his mercies never come to an end; they are new every morning; great is your faithfulness."

3. Isaiah 43:18- 19

"Remember not the former things, nor consider the things of old. Behold, I am doing a new thing; now it springs forth, do you not perceive it? I will make a way in the wilderness and rivers in the desert."

4. Romans 8:31

ADVERTISEMENT

"What then shall we say to these things? If God is for us, who can be against us?"

5. Malachi 4:2

"But for you who fear my name, the sun of righteousness shall rise with healing in its wings. You shall go out leaping like calves from the stall."

6. Jeremiah 29:11

"For I know the plans I have for you, declares the Lord, plans for welfare and not for evil, to give you a future and a hope."

ADVERTISEMENT

7. Psalm 121:1–8

"A Song of Ascents. I lift up my eyes to the hills. From where does my help come? My help comes from the Lord, who made heaven and earth. He will not let your foot be moved; he who keeps you will not slumber. Behold, he who keeps Israel will neither slumber nor sleep. The Lord is your keeper; the Lord is your shade on your right hand. …"

8. Ezekiel 36:26

"And I will give you a new heart, and a new spirit I will put within you…"

ADVERTISEMENT

9. Revelation 21:4

"He will wipe away every tear from their eyes, and death shall be no more, neither shall there be mourning, nor crying, nor pain anymore, for the former things have passed away."

10. Psalm 40:3

"He put a new song in my mouth, a song of praise to our God. Many will see and fear, and put their trust in the Lord."

11. Philippians 3:13–14

ADVERTISEMENT

"Brothers, I do not consider that I have made it my own. But one thing I do: forgetting what lies behind and straining forward to what lies ahead, I press on toward the goal for the prize of the upward call of God in Christ Jesus."

12. Ephesians 4:22–24

"Put off your old self, which belongs to your former manner of life and is corrupt through deceitful desires, and to be renewed in the spirit of your minds, and to put on the new self, created after the likeness of God in true righteousness and holiness."

13. Luke 7:47

"Therefore I tell you, her sins, which are many, are forgiven-for she loved much. But he who is forgiven little loves little."

ADVERTISEMENT

14. Job 8:7

"And though your beginning was small, your latter days will be very great."

15. Revelation 21:5

"And he who was seated on the throne said, "Behold, I am making all things new." Also, he said, "Write this down, for these words are trustworthy and true."

Happy new month!
