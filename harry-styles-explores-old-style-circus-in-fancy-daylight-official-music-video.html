Amid the looming end of his multi-year Love on Tour, Harry Styles dropped the official music video of “Daylight.” The said visualizer serves as the fifth brand-new tape from the global pop icon’s third studio album, Harry’s House.

Harry Styles Releases Brand-New “Daylight” Music Video

In an Instagram post dated July 20, 2023, the former One Direction member announced the visualizer’s release. As stated in the description, the music video was “made with love by a creative team from Ukraine.”

Styles kicks off the music video sporting a black satin ensemble which looks like a French clown costume. As the song continues to play, he casually explores the circus and interacts with acrobats and trapeze acts. 

The “As It Was” singer also gets shot out of cannon while wearing a yellow-feathered outfit, proceeding to defy gravity in a few seconds. Furthermore, elsewhere in the visualizer, he rode a huge unicycle and attempted to be a stunt-oriented artist.

After the inception of 2023, he released music videos for tracks “Late Night Talking” and “Music for a Sushi Restaurant.” Moreover, as he added more dates for Love On Tour, he snatched the Album of the Year trophy at this year’s Grammy Awards.

“Daylight” Cements the Singer’s Playful, Eccentric Artistic Persona

With the official release of the music video, Styles has only reinforced his bizarre yet whimsical artistic identity. From his unusual lyrics to its vibrant visual themes, the solo singer is indeed making a mark in the music industry.

The concept of an old-style traveling circus is not unexpected from Styles. Nonetheless, despite his previous quirky but pleasant visualizers, the singer still managed to make this latest one unique.

“Daylight” official video is probably the last one that the pop singer will drop from the album. Thus, it’s not a shock that fans are both emotional and sentimental about it. The same also reminds them that Styles’ Harry’s House era will soon come to an end.

“I hate even thinking this album has come to an end!! Thank you for everything you have done for us !!” one fan wrote in the comments. “I’m gonna miss you can’t wait to see what you come up with next!!”

At present,  Styles is in the middle of his Love On Tour. The “Watermelon Sugar” singer will be performing at RCF Arena in Italy on July 22. He will then move on to Madison Square Garden in New York on September 1.
